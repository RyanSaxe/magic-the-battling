## Overview

The way the format works, like an autobattler, is players alternate between [drafting](doc:draft) cards and playing a short game of magic (called a [battle](doc:battle)). After each battle, the loser gets [poison counter(s)](doc:game-pieces#poison). Then all players draft again. This loop continues until only one player remains as the winner.

## Beginning the Game

At the beginning of the game, starting hand size is set to 3, all players are dealt 7 cards and a [treasure](doc:game-pieces#treasures), and they enter the [build phase](doc:build) at stage 3, round 1.

You can always view the current stage and round in the top left corner of the game. Your stage is always equal to your starting hand size, and there are three rounds in each stage.

## The Game Loop

Each round goes through the following phases, in order:

- **[Draft](doc:draft)** - Swap cards in your pool with cards in a pack to make your pool stronger.
- **[Build](doc:build)** - Build the best starting hand you can make from your pool with 3 basic lands of your choice starting on the battlefield untapped.
- **[Battle](doc:battle)** - Play a quick mini-game of Magic with 10 life and no libraries.
- **[Reward](doc:reward)** - Collect treasures and bonus cards. Every 3 Reward phases hand size increases.

At the end of this loop, if there are two or more players alive, then all players move to the [Draft](doc:draft) phase of the next stage/round.
