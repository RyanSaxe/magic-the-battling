## Overview

Magic: The Battling is an autobattler format for Magic: The Gathering. Players alternate between drafting cards and playing short battles. After each battle, the loser gets poison counters. The last player standing wins!

The game loop repeats across stages:

- **Draft** — Pick cards from a pack to strengthen your pool
- **Build** — Assemble your starting hand from your pool
- **Battle** — Play a quick mini-game of Magic against an opponent
- **Reward** — Collect treasures and bonus cards

Each stage increases your hand size, letting you play bigger and more powerful hands as the game progresses. Spend treasures wisely during the draft to reroll for better cards — they're your most valuable resource.
