## Overview

The way the format works, like an autobattler, is players alternate between drafting cards and playing a short game of magic (called a battle). After each battle, the loser gets poison counter(s). Then all players draft again. This loop continues until only one player remains as the winner.

## Beginning the Game

At the beginning of the game, starting hand size is set to 3, all players are dealt 7 cards and a treasure, and they enter the build phase at stage 3, round 1. You can always view the current stage-round in the top left of the game.

Your stage is always equal to your starting hand size, and there are three rounds in each stage. A round consists of four phases described below.

## The Loop: One Round

- **Draft** - Swap cards in your pool with cards in a pack to make your pool stronger.
- **Build** - Build the best starting hand you can make from your pool with 3 basic lands of your choice starting on the battlefield untapped.
- **Battle** - Play a quick mini-game of Magic with 10 life and no libraries.
- **Reward** - Collect treasures and bonus cards. Every 3 Reward phases hand size increases.

The loop above will continue until only one play remains as the winner!
